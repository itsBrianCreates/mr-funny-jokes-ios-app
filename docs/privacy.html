Privacy Policy

Mr. Funny Jokes

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Mr. Funny Jokes ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Mr. Funny Jokes does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Do Not Collect

Mr. Funny Jokes is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Browsing history or app usage patterns
  • Contact information or address book data
  • Photos, videos, or media files

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores certain preferences and data locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Joke Ratings: Your ratings (likes/dislikes) for jokes you've interacted with
  • App Preferences: Your selected settings and preferences within the app
  • Viewing History: Information about jokes you've seen to enhance your experience

This locally stored data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to us or any third party
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Under your complete control

Home Screen Widget

Mr. Funny Jokes includes a home screen widget feature. The widget:

  • Displays joke content on your home screen
  • Does not collect any additional data
  • Uses the same local storage as the main app
  • Does not transmit any information

Third-Party Services

The App retrieves joke content from our cloud database (Firebase Firestore). This connection:

  • Only downloads joke content to display in the app
  • Does not upload any personal information
  • Does not track or identify individual users
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

No User Accounts

Mr. Funny Jokes does not require or offer user account creation. You can use all features of the app without providing any personal information or creating an account.

No Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not share any data with advertising networks or partners.

Children's Privacy

Mr. Funny Jokes does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect personal information from any users, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our services. All user preferences are stored securely on your device using Apple's built-in secure storage mechanisms.

Your Rights

You have complete control over any data stored locally on your device:

  • Access: View your stored preferences within the app settings
  • Deletion: Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Your data stays on your device and is not transferable

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
