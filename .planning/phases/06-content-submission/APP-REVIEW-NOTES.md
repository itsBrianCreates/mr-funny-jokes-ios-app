# App Review Notes

**Purpose:** Copy/paste the content below the line into App Store Connect's "Notes for Review" field.

---

Mr. Funny Jokes delivers native iOS integration through Siri Shortcuts, Home Screen and Lock Screen Widgets, Local Notifications, and a Monthly Top 10 Leaderboard. Here's how to test each feature:

SIRI SHORTCUTS
1. Open the Shortcuts app on your device
2. Search for "Mr. Funny Jokes" - the "Tell Me a Joke" shortcut appears automatically (no setup required)
3. Tap the shortcut to hear Siri speak a random joke
4. Alternative: Say "Hey Siri, tell me a joke from Mr. Funny Jokes"
5. Expected result: Siri speaks the joke aloud without opening the app

HOME SCREEN WIDGETS
1. Long-press on the Home Screen and tap the "+" button in the top-left corner
2. Search for "Mr. Funny Jokes" in the widget gallery
3. Add any size widget (Small, Medium, or Large)
4. Expected result: Widget displays the Joke of the Day with character branding and updates daily

LOCK SCREEN WIDGETS
1. Long-press on the Lock Screen and tap "Customize"
2. Select "Lock Screen" to edit
3. Tap the widget area above or below the time
4. Search for and add a Mr. Funny Jokes widget (circular, rectangular, or inline)
5. Expected result: Widget displays character icon or joke text depending on widget style

NOTIFICATIONS
1. Open the app and navigate to the Settings tab
2. Enable "Joke of the Day" notifications
3. Grant notification permission when prompted by iOS
4. Expected result: Daily notification at 9:00 AM with a joke preview
5. Tap the notification to open the app and view the full joke

MONTHLY TOP 10 LEADERBOARD
1. Open the app and navigate to the Home tab
2. Scroll down to the "Monthly Top 10" section
3. Expected result: Displays the 10 highest-rated jokes from the past 30 days, ranked by community ratings
4. Tap any joke to view it in detail and see its rating breakdown

ADDITIONAL NOTES
- All features work offline using cached jokes after the initial app launch
- The app includes 5 unique character personas (Mr. Funny, Mr. Potty, Mr. Bad, Mr. Love, Mr. Sad), each with distinct humor styles
- Users can rate jokes with emoji reactions (1-5 scale), browse by category, and share jokes with friends

Thank you for reviewing Mr. Funny Jokes!
