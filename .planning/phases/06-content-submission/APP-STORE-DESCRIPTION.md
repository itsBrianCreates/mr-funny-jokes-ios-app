Warning: May cause uncontrollable groaning and reluctant laughter. Mr. Funny Jokes delivers the best (worst?) dad jokes, dark humor, pickup lines, and more - now with Siri and widgets!

ASK SIRI FOR A JOKE
"Hey Siri, tell me a joke from Mr. Funny Jokes" - hear a joke spoken aloud without lifting a finger. Perfect for road trips, boring meetings (muted, of course), or when you just need a laugh.

WIDGETS EVERYWHERE
Add Joke of the Day to your Home Screen or Lock Screen. Wake up to a fresh joke every morning. Small, medium, large - we've got widgets in all sizes, just like our jokes.

DAILY NOTIFICATIONS
Never miss your daily dose of humor. Get a joke delivered to your lock screen every morning at 9 AM. It's like a newspaper, but actually funny.

MEET THE CHARACTERS

Mr. Funny - The classic dad joke master. Puns so bad they're good. Eye rolls guaranteed.

Mr. Potty - For the kid in all of us. Bathroom humor that'll have you flushing with laughter.

Mr. Bad - Dark humor for those with an edge. Not for the faint of heart.

Mr. Love - Pickup lines and romantic quips. Use at your own risk (we're not responsible for any eye rolls from your crush).

Mr. Sad - Melancholy humor for when you need to laugh through the pain. Surprisingly relatable.

FEATURES YOU'LL LOVE
- Swipe through endless jokes with satisfying card animations
- Rate jokes with emoji reactions (from one groan to five laughs)
- See what's trending in the Monthly Top 10 leaderboard
- Filter by category: Dad Jokes, Knock-Knock, or Pickup Lines
- Copy and share jokes with friends (or enemies, we don't judge)
- Works offline after first launch - jokes cached for airplane mode entertainment
- Haptic feedback that makes every swipe feel just right

NATIVE iOS INTEGRATION
Mr. Funny Jokes isn't just another joke app - it's built from the ground up for iOS:
- Siri Shortcuts for hands-free jokes
- Home Screen and Lock Screen Widgets
- Smart notifications that respect Do Not Disturb
- Designed for iPhone with iOS 18+

Download now and join thousands of groan-worthy joke enthusiasts. Your friends and family will thank you. Or block you. Either way, mission accomplished.
